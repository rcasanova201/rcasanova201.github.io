How to make Guacamole:


Have a hankering for a yummy snack, and have some avocadoes hanging around? Why, you can make some delicious and nutritous guac! Break out some tortilla chips and follow these steps:

  1. Pick some fresh and ripe avocadoes
  2. Do a rough chop on half an onion, 3 cloves of garlic, and a few sprigs of cilantro
  3. Get a medium sized bowl, cut your avocado(es) in half, and with a spoon scoop out the fruit of both halves into the bowl
  4. Add your previously chopped ingredients
  5. Mix and incorporate all of the contents in bowl, adding a couple drizzles of olive oil and salt (to taste)

Et voila! You have some homemade guac waiting to be enjoyed!

Pro tip:Store guac in mason jar, WITH the seed inside (old grandma trick so the guac won't spoil). Try it!
